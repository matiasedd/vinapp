Usage: vina++ [-iamxrch] [ARCHIVER] [MEMBERS]

Options:

   -i		insert members into archiver
   -a		insert members into archiver if they are newer
   -m		move source member to immediately after target member
   -x		extract members from archiver
   -r		remove members from archiver
   -c		list members in archiver
   -h		show this help message and exit
